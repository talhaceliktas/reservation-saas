## ðŸ‡¬ðŸ‡§ BookIt - Reservation SaaS Platform

BookIt is a modern, full-stack reservation and appointment management platform designed for small and medium-sized businesses. It allows businesses to manage their services, staff, and appointments while providing a seamless booking experience for their customers.

### ðŸš€ Features

- **Multi-Language Support**: Fully localized in English, Turkish, German, Spanish, and French.
- **Authentication**: Secure user authentication and management via Supabase Auth.
- **Organization Management**: Create and switch between multiple business organizations.
- **Staff Management**: Invite team members, assign roles (Owner, Admin, Staff), and manage permissions.
- **Services & Pricing**: Create, edit, and delete services with custom durations and pricing.
- **Dashboard**: A responsive dashboard for managing all aspects of the business.
- **Mobile-First Design**: Fully responsive layout with a dedicated mobile sidebar and navigation.
